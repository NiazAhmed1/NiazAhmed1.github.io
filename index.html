Interesting Facts About Dogs

  • Dogs have a remarkable sense of smell, with an estimated 300 million olfactory receptors in their noses, compared to only 6 million in humans. This makes them particularly skilled at detecting scents, which is why they are often used for tasks such as detecting drugs, explosives, and even medical conditions like cancer
  • Dogs have been domesticated for over 15,000 years.
  • Dogs can understand up to 250 words and gestures.
  • Dogs have a unique ability to form deep bonds with their human companions and provide comfort, companionship, and even assistance to people in need.
  • Dogs have a sense of time and can tell how long their owners have been gone.
